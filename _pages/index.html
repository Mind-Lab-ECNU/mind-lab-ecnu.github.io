ECNU Mind Lab

About Us

ECNU Mind Lab is an interdisciplinary research group working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, education, cognition, and human-centered computing. We build practical research systems, design interactive tools for learning, and study how intelligent methods can be translated into real educational and social impact.

Research Focus

  • Educational AI: question generation, automated assessment, and curriculum-aware interactive systems.
  • Human-Centered Intelligence: interfaces and workflows that connect model capabilities with real users.
  • Cognitive and Multimodal Computing: structured reasoning, multimodal analysis, and interpretable decision support.
  • Research Software Prototyping: deployable systems for demonstrations, pilots, and collaborative experimentation.

Join Us

We welcome students, research assistants, and collaborators who are interested in educational AI, multimodal systems, and interactive research platforms. If you would like to work on software demos, publications, or prototype deployment, please contact the lab and introduce your background and interests.
